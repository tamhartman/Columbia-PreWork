This here's the Devil's Music

Teddybears are a swedish rock sensation that have swept the globe with their hot beats, fresh shapes, and bear headed awesomeness. Their music is infused with pop, rock, hip-hop, electronica, reggae, punk, and wild space robots. Beginning their discography as grindcore group Skull, Teddybears have been shaking butts since 1991. They've done collaborations with Robyn, Iggy Pop, Eve, Mad Cobra, Elephant Man, Neneh Cherry, The Flaming Lips, and more.
